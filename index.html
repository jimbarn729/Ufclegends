Frequently Asked Questions

How are the cards delivered? +

Cards are shipped in protective packaging via tracked mail. Delivery costs vary by location and are calculated separately. Please contact us on Telegram for specific delivery quotes.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept USDT on the Solana blockchain. Please send payment to the address provided in your cart. Other cryptocurrencies may be available upon request.

Are the cards officially licensed? +

Yes, all UFC Legends cards are officially licensed by UFC. We work directly with the organization to ensure authenticity and quality.

How do I receive my meme coin bonus? +

After your order is confirmed and payment received, we will send your UFC Legends "UFCL" meme coin bonus to the Solana wallet address you provide.

Can I return or exchange cards? +

Due to the collectible nature of our products, returns and exchanges are only accepted for damaged items. Please contact us within 7 days of receipt for damaged product claims.
